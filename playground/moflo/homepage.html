KNOW YOUR FLOW

moflo analyzes your income and expenses, predicts your future financial health, shows detailed spending patterns, and gives you the information you need to take control of your finances.

How can I stop overdrafting my checking account?

By learning your income and expense patterns, moflo knows if you’re in danger of overdrafting your account and sends you alerts so you can stop getting hit with overdraft fees and keep more of your hard-earned money.

Can I afford an item I want?

Checking your account balance doesn’t answer the question. You still have to calculate upcoming bills. moflo knows your upcoming bills and does the math for you.

Where did my money go?

Budget Apps put expenses into broad categories that aren’t very helpful. moflo groups your expenses into detailed categories that make it more obvious where your money really goes. Or will go.
